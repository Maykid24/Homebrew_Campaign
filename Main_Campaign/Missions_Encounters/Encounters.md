## Encounters:

- Roll 1d20
    - Higher than 18 = Encounter

- If encounter occurs:
    - Roll 1d20
    - 1-10 Bad encounter (Enemy creatures)
    - 11-20 Good encounter (Friendly creatures)

### Good Encounters:
- Carriages passing by
- Random trade wagon
- Friendly creatures (Deer, Fox, etc.)
- Group of travelers
